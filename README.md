# sabbathme 🕯️

*The architecture of holiness in time — the ceasing that builds a palace, set down as a cosmic ordinance.*

sabbathme is two received roots welded into one word: the Hebrew **shabbat** (שַׁבָּת), the seventh-day ceasing whose root *shavat* means *to stop, to desist*, carried with Heschel's reading of the sabbath as a "palace built in time"; and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So sabbathme is the structural fact that holiness can be built into *time* and not only into *space* — through abstention-from-work, structured prayer, sanctified meals, and presence kept by appointment. Where the West built its sacred into temples of stone, sabbathme names the other axis: time as the medium a holiness is constructed in, the sundown-edge that divides one kind of hour from another.

sabbathme nests at every scale — daily prayer at day-scale, sabbath at week-scale, the liturgical year at year-scale — the same architecture rhymed up and down the orders of time. Its light is rest made into affirmation rather than lack, the reclaimed edge in an attention-economy that never stops; its shadow is the cage that hardens rule above mercy and mistakes the keeping of the form for the arrival of the holy. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.60/10** — among Phase 4's strongest.
